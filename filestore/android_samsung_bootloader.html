Unlock Bootloader

1.    Enable Developer Options

            Settings > About phone > Software information > Press build number 7 times

2.    Enable OEM Unlocking

            Settings > Developer options > OEM Unlocking > Enable

            If this option is greyed out with "Bootloader already unlocked", perform steps 3 and 4, then press volume up.

3.    Turn Off Phone

            Hold volume down + power, then select power off

4.    Enter Download Mode

            Hold volume down + volume up whilst plugging phone into PC. Keep holding both volume buttons until warning screen.

5.    Enter Unlock Mode

            Hold volume up until black screen. When you release volume up, "Unlock bootloader?" message will show.

6.    Unlock Bootloader

            Hold volume up, then without releasing, also hold volume down. At warning screen, release both buttons. Then press volume up. Finally, hold volume up + sleep.
