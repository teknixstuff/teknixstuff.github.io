Lock Bootloader

1.    Turn Off Phone

            Hold power, then select power off

2.    Enter Download Mode

            Hold volume down + volume up whilst plugging phone into PC. Keep holding both volume buttons until warning screen.

3.    Enter Lock Mode

            Hold volume up until black screen. When you release volume up, "Lock bootloader?" message will show.

4.    Lock Bootloader

            Hold volume up, then without releasing, also hold volume down. At warning screen, release both buttons. Then press volume up. Finally, hold volume up + sleep until SAMSUNG logo.
